Inkomoko Early Warning System

An ML-powered platform that monitors small and growing businesses across East Africa, predicting risk, revenue, and employment outcomes to enable timely, data-driven interventions.

Risk Prediction

Classifies enterprises into HIGH, MEDIUM, and LOW risk tiers with continuous risk scores across 3, 6, and 12-month horizons.

Revenue Forecasting

Projects future revenue for each enterprise, enabling proactive financial planning and cashflow gap identification.

Employment Impact

Predicts jobs created and jobs at risk, helping safeguard livelihoods and plan workforce interventions.
